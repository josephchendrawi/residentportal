Key Features

Here are some of the amazing things that you will get with Resident Portal...

Your Own Property Website

Have your very own property community website where owners and residents can get latest updates as well as being in touch with your property management team with ease. This website serves as a central collaboration point between all parties. With a website, it not only streamlines your work operations but also exhibits your commitment in providing the best to the owners and residents. And better yet, your website is responsive – meaning it works on both desktop or mobile devices.

Announcements

Reaching out to owners and residents has never been easier. An informative digital notice board made available on your very own property website allows you to effectively communicate updates and latest happenings to the owners and residents effortlessly. Alternately, send out e-newsletters via the portal to keep them informed and updated.

Facilities Reservation

Allow owners and residents to make reservations with ease online or through their mobile phones for function rooms, conference hall, squash courts, tennis courts, basketball courts or any other shared common facilities.

Complaints & Defects Management

A full-fledge inquiry, complaints and defects management system whereby owners and residents themselves can login to submit inquiries or complaints. Having a centralised repository also enables owners and residents to view the status of their inquiries or complaints online without the need to always contact the property management team.

Online Bill Payments

Provide the flexibility for the owners and residents to view their statement of account and pay for outstanding bills online using their credit cards or via online banking (e.g. Maybank2u, CIMB Clicks, Hong Leong Connect, etc). Resident Portal also automatically sends out email reminders to both owners and residents for due items.
