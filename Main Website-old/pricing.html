Frequently Asked Questions

Q. How soon can I start using Resident Portal?
Your will have access to Resident Portal instantly! As soon as you register, you will receive an email with your access details and you will be able to start using the Resident Portal immediately.

Q. Do I need to install anything to use Resident Portal?
No installation is needed and no technical knowledge is required either. To use Resident Portal, all you need to do is to open your browser, key in the URL and you can immediately start using it. Resident Portal was designed with simplicity in mind so that non-techies and ordinary users can use it. In fact, all you need to know is how to use a mouse and keyboard.

Q. How do I get training for Resident Portal?
Once you have registered, you will have access to our library of online video tutorials to assist you to use Resident Portal. You will also have access to a quick guide to help you get started quickly and immediately. Plus, if you need additional support, you can contact our support team for assistance.

Q. How do I get support for Resident Portal?
Just email us at support@residentportal.my or should you need to speak to us, dial us at +603-86885821. On top of that, we will be establishing a network of support engineers located throughout Malaysia to assist our valued customers.
